Lion

Lions are the only cats that live in groups.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 184–208 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 10 – 14 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands and savannas

A group, or pride, can be up to 30 lions, depending on how much food and water is available. Female lions are the main hunters.A lion's roar can be heard up to eight kilometres away.
